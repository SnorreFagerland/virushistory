<h2>The Cookie Monster</h2>

The Cookie Monster was not a virus or even (much) of a trojan - it was a prank that would annoy users by locking the terminal and ask for a cookie.
If you typed "COOKIE" it would let you continue.

However, the prank had ripple effects - copycats started showing up. 
There are stories of clones that worked the same way but asked different questions:<br>

* Susan Ford (wants a DATE)<br>
* Richard Nixon (wants a PARDON)

Then malware for Atari ST, DOS, Win32, and even VBA macros came that played on the same meme.

The Cookie Monster also made its way into the movie "Hackers" (1995) where a Cookie "virus" is loose.
